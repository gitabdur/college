About :

Bachelor of Engineering in Production Engineering is four-year full-time course degree offered by Jadavpur University, Kolkata and approved by All India Council for Technical Education.
Famous researcher and academician Late Prof Amitava Bhattacharyya was the founder of this Dept and it took birth in the year 1980. Production Engineering Department is a pioneering Centre for learning of Manufacturing Sciences and Management in the academic map of India. The department is having a very good track record in respect of employment of the students. Majority of the students are getting job in reputed companies and many of them even are getting more than one job from campus interview itself. Due to its excellent performance in teaching, students from different countries (Jordon, Iraq, Netherland etc) are coming to this department for perusing their higher studies. Nevertheless, students from this department are also going to abroad for higher studies under student exchange program of the University. This department is having an exceptional track record in the field of R&D and in fact as per Scopus database, research performance (in terms of publication per faculty and citation per faculty) is among the top ranking departments considering all Mechanical, Manufacturing, Production & Industrial Engg Departments of different leading IITs and Universities of our nation.
